Observability only becomes useful when it helps operators make decisions quickly. Platform teams need enough telemetry to spot saturation, drift, and failure trends without producing dashboards nobody trusts.

## Focus Areas

- Metrics tied to saturation, scaling, and error rates.
- Logs that support incident reconstruction rather than raw volume.
- Alerts with clear ownership and action paths.

A good baseline uses CloudWatch effectively, but the bigger win comes from linking signals to concrete operational playbooks.
